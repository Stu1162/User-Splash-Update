StorkUp is a brand new social shopping platform for mums

  • Discover products from over 250 parenting websites and follow your favourites

    StorkUp helps mums navigate the world of kids’ products. It’s a unique shopping experience where our growing community of mums can discover, collect, and buy from an entirely crowd-sourced product catalogue of fabulous products and stores.

  • Create and share lists of products for your little ones

    Save your favourite products into handy lists and share them with your friends! Create as many lists as you need and organise your products your way.

  • See what’s trending across 19 different children’s and maternity categories

    Are you looking for a new pram? Some amazing toys? Quirky decorations? Are you mad about flowers, frogs or zigzags? Whatever you're looking for, StorkUp has you covered.

  • Get answers to your parenting questions from mums like you

    Being a parent is all about learning on the job, but everyone needs a helping hand sometimes. Share experiences, swap stories and dip into the wisdom of other mums and dads in the StorkUp community.
